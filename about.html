About Me

I'm a transplant to Chicago from Nashville, TN and have walked the globe with Peace Corps and a stint in Africa.

A learner and a talker, I find new adventures all the time and keep looking for a challenge. I found my to gateway language into programming in HTML/CSS. Then I found SASS, Javascript, Rails, and Ruby.

How can anyone trek back to an office job after getting to Ruby and Rails? A few Girl Develop It classes, amazing coding friends, and some meetups later, I earned #yeswecode scholarship to Dev Bootcamp.

I love the full stack, from the HTML to Ruby and databases in the back, and even jumping into the middle of DevOps Days Chicago. Pairing and testing also
