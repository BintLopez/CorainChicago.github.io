About Me

I am a web developer who leans towards the backend and loves to pair. My love of logic, reasoning and problem solving led me to the promised land of coding after a long trek through Peace Corps, teaching, teaching in a near Peace Corps-like situation, finally back to the US into non-profit program development, and finally to the gateway languages of HTML/CSS, Javascript, and then Ruby and Rails.

How can anyone trek back to an office job after getting to Ruby and Rails? Not me, so a hop, skip, a few Girl Develop It classes, two amazing coding friends, and some meetups later, I found myself with a #yeswecode scholarship to Dev Bootcamp.
